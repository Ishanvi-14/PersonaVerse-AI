# Quality Bar & Guardrails

PersonaVerse output is acceptable ONLY if:
1. **Persona Recognition:** A human identifies the persona without being told.
2. **Platform Native-ness:** LinkedIn sounds professional; WhatsApp sounds personal.
3. **Linguistic Fluency:** Hinglish feels naturally spoken, not machine-translated.
4. **Historical Alignment:** Output aligns with the user's "Digital Soul" memory.

Failure on any dimension requires immediate rejection or regeneration.
